# Goal Setting

## Notes


It doesn't matter how fast your going, if you don't have a clear direction, you will never get to where you want to go.
Goal setting is a tool to give our direction, inspiration, and be the core principle from which we derive our daily actions. 
The goal has to derive itself, or at least be aligned with your (Related:: [[Core Values]])
Only with the combination of day to day productivity, with our goals in mind, we would actually be productive.
A life mission has to be clear (Aka:: [[Clarity]]), and inspirational, so that it would answer the question "how do I know when I have succeeded", such as "I will become a doctor in five years".
