# Complexity

## Notes

Complexity is when an idea is harder to understand, or to implement. Complexity is not necessarily a bad thing because complex systems can be highly beneficials because they allow the combination between multiple viewpoints, taking into account many variables and giving a nuanced result. 

A common issue with complexity is the increased complexity in human life. Innovation, education and productivity require us to constantly improve, making it harder and harder as time goes by because of (Related:: [[Diminishing Returns]]). If 1000 years ago, just be being literate you could have been the most successful merchant, 100 years ago you needed to have basic math, and today you need computer skills, and soft skills, etc.
Sometimes it leads to the [[red queen problem]], that you need to run nonstop just to stay in the same place.
